Wedding Butler

- Under construction -

Our initial idea was a simple wedding seating app that would allow for wedding hosts to provide guest lists, and be able to algorithmically assign seats based on each guestâ€™s like or dislike of another guest, with the intent being to provide the appropriate amount of space between them. However, we believed we could do much more than just seating arrangements.

Our current intent is to make an all-in-one wedding planning app that would be able to, having been given the budget for the wedding, make suggestions for various services, such as catering, venue booking, or floral arrangements, so as to best fit the provided budget. Our seating arrangement planner will only be a component of this application. In regards to service suggestion, we could provide other details for each given service provider, with the intent being to help our customers get weddings planned out to be under budget and ahead of schedule.

In the real world, we already have professional wedding planners. The goal of this application will be to provide a means for the user to achieve semi-professional results, without necessarily needing to add on additional wedding planner fees to their budget.
